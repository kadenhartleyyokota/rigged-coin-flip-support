Introduction

Rigged Coin Flip ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application. By using Rigged Coin Flip, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Important: We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All app data is stored locally on your device only.

Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults:

  • Coin Flip History: Records of your coin flips including timestamp and outcome
  • App Settings: Your selected coin type, default outcome preference, and Fair Mode setting
  • Statistics: Total number of flips and usage patterns

This data:

  • Remains on your device only
  • Is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Cannot be accessed by us or anyone else

No Personal Information

We do NOT collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Apple ID or any authentication credentials
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Analytics or usage tracking data
  • Any other personally identifiable information

How We Use Your Information

Since all data is stored locally on your device, we do not have access to or use your information in any way. The app uses your locally stored data solely to:

  • Display your flip history and statistics within the app
  • Maintain your app preferences and settings
  • Provide the core coin flipping functionality

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only

All app data is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is protected by your device's security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)
  • Is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Cannot be synced across devices

No Cloud Storage

The app does NOT use:

  • iCloud or CloudKit
  • Any remote servers or databases
  • Any third-party cloud services

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties.

The app does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks. Your data stays on your device and is never shared with anyone.

Your Privacy Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: You can view your flip history and settings within the app at any time
  • Deletion: You can clear your flip history through the app settings, or delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • Control: All data is stored locally on your device under your control

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. All data is stored locally on the device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
