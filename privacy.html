Introduction

Rigged Coin Flip ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. By using Rigged Coin Flip, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you sign in with Apple, we collect:

  • Apple ID: Used for authentication and iCloud sync
  • Name: Optional, provided by Apple Sign In
  • Email: Optional, provided by Apple Sign In (may be a private relay email)

Usage Data

We automatically collect the following data to provide app functionality:

  • Coin Flip History: Records of your coin flips including timestamp and outcome
  • App Settings: Your selected coin type and default outcome preference
  • Statistics: Total number of flips and usage patterns
Important: All usage data is stored exclusively in your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to our servers.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide and improve the app experience:

  • Authenticate your identity via Apple Sign In
  • Sync your settings and flip history across your devices via iCloud
  • Display your flip statistics and history within the app
  • Maintain your app preferences

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

App data is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults and is never transmitted to external servers except as described below.

iCloud Storage (Required)

Your flip history and app settings are stored in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit service. This data is:

  • Encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Only accessible by you across your devices
  • Never accessible by us or any third parties
  • Stored in Apple's secure cloud infrastructure

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Authentication via Apple's secure Sign in with Apple service
  • No data is stored on external servers
  • CloudKit data is protected by your Apple ID credentials

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or transmit your personal information to any third parties.

The only external service we use is:

  • Apple CloudKit: For syncing your data across your devices. This data remains in your personal iCloud account and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can view your flip history and settings within the app at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete your flip history through the app settings
  • Account Deletion: You can sign out at any time, which will remove your local data. Your iCloud data will remain in your iCloud account and can be deleted through iCloud settings
  • Data Portability: Your data is stored in standard formats in your iCloud account

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
