About Rigged Coin Flip

Rigged Coin Flip is a fun iOS app that lets you control the outcome of coin flips. Choose from 8 different coins, set your preferred outcome, and use the secret volume button trick to change the result on the fly!

How to Use

  1. Choose Your Coin: Go to Settings and select from 8 different coin types
  2. Set Default Outcome: Pick which side you want the coin to land on by default
  3. Tap to Flip: Tap the coin on the main screen to start the flip animation
  4. Secret Control: Press the volume UP button while the coin is flipping to get the opposite result
  5. View History: Check your flip history and statistics in the History tab

Troubleshooting

Volume button not working

  • Make sure you're pressing the volume UP button (not DOWN)
  • Press the button while the coin is actively flipping
  • Volume button detection only works on physical devices, not simulators

iCloud sync not working

  • Make sure you're signed in with Apple Sign In
  • Check that iCloud is enabled in Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí iCloud
  • Ensure you have an active internet connection

App crashes or freezes

  • Try force-closing the app and reopening it
  • Restart your device
  • Check for app updates in the App Store
