About Rigged Coin Flip

Rigged Coin Flip is a fun iOS app that lets you control the outcome of coin flips. Use Fair Mode for truly random flips, or adjust the bias slider to control the probability. Press the volume button during a flip for instant control!

How to Use

  1. Fair Mode: Toggle Fair Mode in Settings for truly random flips, or turn it off to use bias control
  2. Set Bias: When Fair Mode is off, use the bias slider to control the probability (50-100%)
  3. Choose Default: Pick which side you want the coin to land on by default (Heads or Tails)
  4. Tap to Flip: Tap the coin on the main screen to start the flip animation
  5. Volume Override: Press the volume UP button while the coin is flipping to get the opposite result
  6. View History: Check your flip history and statistics in the History tab

Troubleshooting

Volume button not working

  • Make sure you're pressing the volume UP button (not DOWN)
  • Press the button while the coin is actively flipping
  • Volume button detection only works on physical devices, not simulators

App crashes or freezes

  • Try force-closing the app and reopening it
  • Restart your device
  • Check for app updates in the App Store
  • If the problem persists, try clearing your flip history in Settings

Lost my flip history

  • Flip history is stored locally on your device only
  • If you uninstall the app, all data is permanently deleted
  • Data cannot be recovered after deletion
  • There is no cloud backup or sync feature

Privacy & Data

Rigged Coin Flip takes your privacy seriously. All your data is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. No account creation or sign-in is required. Your flip history and settings are yours alone.
